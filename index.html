Welcome!

I'm Jack Bosco, a Computer Science graduate from Washington and Lee University (Class of 2024) and a current Master's student at Columbia University (Class of 2026). I am a dedicated software engineer with expertise in AI, machine learning, NLP, and predictive analytics, backed by a strong foundation in mathematics and computer science.

Academic Background

I graduated from W&L with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, maintaining an overall GPA of 3.88 and a major GPA of 3.96. Currently, I am pursuing my Master of Science at Columbia University, where I continue to deepen my understanding of canonical Machine Learning, Deep Learning, NLP and Explainable AI.

Research and Contributions

My hands-on experience includes research collaborations at NYU Langone Health, where I co-authored a study on predicting knee alignments in robotic-assisted surgery, and gave a short talk at the 2024 ISTA Annual Congress.

Leadership and Community Involvement

Professional Goals
